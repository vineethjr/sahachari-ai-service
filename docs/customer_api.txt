SAHACHARI CUSTOMER GUIDE
========================

CREATING AN ACCOUNT
Sahachari is open to everyone. To create a new account, download the Sahachari app and register with your name, email address, mobile number, password, and your home address with pincode. Your password must be at least 6 characters long. Once registered, you can log in immediately and start shopping.

LOGGING IN
Open the Sahachari app and enter your registered email address and password to log in. If your email or password is incorrect, you will see an error asking you to check your details.

YOUR PROFILE
After logging in, you can view your profile details including your name, email, address, mobile number and pincode from the Profile section of the app.

BROWSING PRODUCTS
You can browse all available products on Sahachari from the Products section. You can search for a specific product by name, for example search "apple" to find apple products. You can also filter products by category such as Fruits, Vegetables, Dairy, Spices or Beverages. Each product shows its name, price, and any active discounts.

ADDING ITEMS TO CART
To add an item to your cart, select the product and choose the quantity you want. If the item is already in your cart, adding it again will increase the quantity automatically. Your cart is saved so you can continue shopping and come back to it.

VIEWING YOUR CART
You can view everything in your cart from the Cart section of the app. Your cart shows each item, the quantity, and the total amount.

REMOVING ITEMS FROM CART
To remove an item from your cart, go to your Cart, find the item and tap Remove. You can also update the quantity of any item directly from the cart.

PLACING AN ORDER
When you are ready to order, go to your cart and tap Checkout. You will need to provide your delivery address including street, city, pincode, and phone number. You can also add delivery notes such as "leave at door" or "call on arrival". Once you confirm, your order is placed and your cart is cleared automatically. If your cart has items from multiple stores, separate orders are created for each store automatically but they all share one checkout session.

ORDER CANCELLATION
You can cancel an order as long as it has not been picked up for delivery yet. Once the delivery partner has picked up your order, it cannot be cancelled. To cancel, go to your Orders screen, select the order, and tap Cancel. If you paid online, a refund will be processed within 2 to 3 business days.

TRACKING YOUR ORDER
You can check the status of your order anytime from the Orders screen in the app or by asking the Sahachari chatbot "what is my order status". Orders go through the following stages: Placed, Ready, Accepted, Picked Up, Delivered. If a delivery attempt fails, the status will show as Failed.

DELIVERY INFORMATION
Sahachari delivers to serviceable pincodes in your area. Estimated delivery time is 30 to 45 minutes after your order is confirmed. Delivery charges vary by location and are shown at checkout. You can also choose Self Pickup to collect your order yourself at no delivery charge.

PAYMENT METHODS
Sahachari currently supports Cash on Delivery where you pay when your order arrives, and Self Pickup where you collect the order yourself. More payment options may be added in future.

PRODUCTS AND AVAILABILITY
Sahachari offers fresh vegetables, fruits, dairy products, spices, beverages, and home services such as dishwashing. Product availability depends on your local store. If a product is not found, it may not be available in your area at this time.

GETTING HELP
If you have any questions or issues that the chatbot cannot help with, please contact Sahachari support through the Help section of the app. Our team is available during business hours.
