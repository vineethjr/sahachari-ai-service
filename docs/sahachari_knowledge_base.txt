WHAT IS SAHACHARI?
================================================================================
SHORT ANSWER: Sahachari is a local grocery delivery and home services platform
serving communities in Kerala, India, especially villages and rural areas.

Sahachari is a local platform that offers grocery delivery and home services
to communities in Kerala, India, focusing especially on village and rural areas.
For groceries, Sahachari delivers fresh vegetables, fruits, dairy products,
spices, and beverages directly to your doorstep. Beyond groceries, Sahachari
also provides bookable home services such as dishwashing and other household
assistance, making it a one-stop platform for everyday household needs.
Sahachari connects local vendors and service providers with customers, offering
fast and affordable solutions. The name "Sahachari" means "companion" or
"fellow traveller" in Malayalam, reflecting the platform's goal of being a
trusted everyday companion for households in Kerala.

================================================================================

You can place an order on Sahachari in two ways:
