## What is Sahachari?
🌿 Sahachari (സഹചാരി) is your trusted everyday grocery and delivery companion for households in Kerala. We connect you directly with local vendors and home producers for fresh, quality products at fair prices.

## What products does Sahachari sell?
Sahachari offers a wide range of products including:
- Fresh vegetables and fruits sourced from local farms
- Leafy greens — spinach, methi, coriander, palak, curry leaves, amaranth
- Dairy products — milk, curd, paneer, butter, ghee
- Grains and rice — various varieties of Kerala rice, wheat flour
- Oils and spices — coconut oil, mustard oil, pepper, cardamom, turmeric
- Groceries and daily essentials — dal, sugar, salt, tea, coffee
- Snacks and ready-to-eat items
- Beverages — fresh juices, buttermilk, tender coconut water
- Homemade food — curries, pickles, snacks from local home kitchens
- Fast food items
- Services — dishwashing, cleaning, sweeping, mopping
- Rental items for household use

## Do you have organic or locally sourced items?
Yes! Sahachari works closely with local farmers and home producers across Kerala. Many of our vegetables, fruits, and homemade food items are locally sourced and grown using traditional farming methods. Look for items described as "local", "organic", or "home-grown" in the product catalogue. Supporting local means fresher food and stronger communities!

## Can you suggest groceries for a small household of 2?
For a small household of 2 people, here's a practical weekly starter list:
- Fresh vegetables: 1 kg onions, 1 kg tomatoes, 500g potatoes, 250g carrots
- Leafy greens: 500g spinach or palak (fresh, lasts 2–3 days)
- Aromatics: 100g garlic, 50g ginger
- Cooking essentials: 1 litre coconut oil, basic spice pack
- Grains: 2–3 kg rice, 500g wheat flour (atta)
- Protein: 500g dal or lentils
- Dairy: 1 litre milk or 400g curd (can be ordered daily)
You can add items to cart by saying "add 1 kg onions" or browse by saying "show me vegetables".

## What services does Sahachari provide apart from groceries?
Apart from groceries and food, Sahachari offers home services:
- Dishwashing / dish wash service — professional kitchen vessel cleaning
- Sweeping service — floor sweeping for homes
- Mopping service — wet mopping and floor cleaning
- General house cleaning service
These can be booked through the chat by saying "book dishwashing" or "show me services" to see available options with pricing.

## How do I place an order on Sahachari?
Ordering is simple — just tell the assistant what you want in natural language:
- Say "I want 2 kg onions" to add to your cart
- Say "Add 500g spinach and 1 kg tomatoes"
- Say "Show me fruits" to browse available fruits
- Say "What's in my cart?" to review your cart
- Say "Checkout" or "Place my order" to complete the purchase
- Say "Track my order" to check delivery status

## What are Sahachari's delivery timings?
Delivery timings depend on your location and the vendor. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before the cut-off time, which varies by vendor. Next-day delivery is also available. Check the app or ask "what are the delivery timings in my area" for accurate information.

## What are the delivery charges?
Delivery charges depend on your order size and location. Many vendors offer free delivery above a minimum order value. Exact delivery charges are shown at checkout. Ask "what are the delivery charges" for more details.

## What payment methods does Sahachari accept?
Sahachari supports multiple payment methods including online payment (UPI, cards, net banking) and cash on delivery in select areas. Payment options are shown at checkout.

## What is the minimum order value?
Minimum order values vary by vendor. Some vendors on Sahachari have no minimum order, while others may require a minimum. This information is shown when you proceed to checkout.

## Can I cancel my order?
Yes, you can cancel your order if it hasn't been picked up yet. Say "cancel my order" in the chat and I'll attempt to cancel it for you. For orders already out for delivery, please contact support.

## How does Sahachari support the local community?
Sahachari (the Malayalam word for "companion") is built to support local farmers, home producers, and small vendors by connecting them directly with households. Every purchase helps sustain local livelihoods and keeps food supply chains short and fresh. Shopping with Sahachari means fresher food and a stronger Kerala community.

## What areas does Sahachari serve?
Sahachari currently serves select areas in Kerala. Coverage is growing. Share your pincode and we'll confirm if delivery is available in your area.

## How do I track my order?
Say "track my order" or "order status" in the chat and I'll show you the latest status of your most recent order, including whether it's placed, accepted, out for delivery, or delivered.

## I forgot what I ordered — can you show me my past orders?
Yes! Say "show my orders" or "order history" and I'll list all your past orders with their status and items.

## How do I add items to my wishlist?
Say "add 1 kg apples to my wishlist" or "save spinach for later". Items in your wishlist can be moved to the cart later by saying "order my wishlist".

## What are the freshness guarantees?
Sahachari sources directly from local vendors and farmers, which means shorter supply chains and fresher products than supermarkets. Leafy vegetables are typically harvested the same day. If you receive anything that is not fresh, please report it through the app for a refund or replacement.
