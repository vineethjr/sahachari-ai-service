SAHACHARI DELIVERY INFORMATION
==============================

HOW DELIVERY WORKS
When you place an order on Sahachari, it is first received by the store. The store prepares your order and marks it as ready. A delivery partner then accepts the job, picks up your order from the store, and delivers it to your address. You will be able to track each stage from the Orders screen in the app.

ORDER STAGES FOR DELIVERY
Your order goes through these stages:
1. Placed - Your order has been received by the store.
2. Ready - The store has packed your order and it is ready for pickup.
3. Accepted - A delivery partner has accepted your order and is on the way to the store.
4. Picked Up - The delivery partner has collected your order from the store and is on the way to you.
5. Delivered - Your order has been successfully delivered to your address.
6. Failed - The delivery attempt was unsuccessful. Sahachari support will contact you in this case.
7. Cancelled - The order was cancelled before delivery.

DELIVERY TIMING
Estimated delivery time is 30 to 45 minutes after your order is confirmed. Actual timing may vary depending on your location, store preparation time, and delivery partner availability.

DELIVERY CHARGES
Delivery charges are calculated based on your pincode and selected delivery location. You can see the exact delivery charge before confirming your order at checkout. If you choose Self Pickup, there is no delivery charge.

SELF PICKUP OPTION
If you prefer, you can select Self Pickup at checkout to collect your order yourself from the nearest Sahachari pickup point. Self Pickup has no delivery charge.

SERVICEABLE AREAS
Sahachari delivers to specific pincodes within its delivery zone. When you register or checkout, enter your pincode to confirm if delivery is available in your area. More areas are being added regularly.

DELIVERY PARTNER
Your order is delivered by a Sahachari delivery partner. Once your order is picked up, you can see the delivery partner details in the Orders screen. If you have any issue with delivery, contact Sahachari support through the app.

FAILED DELIVERY
If delivery fails, for example because you were not available or the address could not be found, the order status will be marked as Failed. Please contact Sahachari support so we can help resolve the issue and arrange redelivery if possible.

CANCELLING BEFORE DELIVERY
You can cancel your order at any time before it is picked up by the delivery partner. Once picked up, cancellation is not possible. Go to the Orders screen, select the order, and tap Cancel. Refunds for prepaid orders are processed within 2 to 3 business days.
