SAHACHARI PLATFORM INFORMATION
===============================

WHAT IS SAHACHARI
Sahachari is a local grocery delivery and home services platform serving communities in India. It connects customers with local stores and service providers for fast, affordable home delivery of everyday essentials. The name Sahachari means companion in Malayalam, reflecting its mission to be a trusted everyday helper for households.

WHAT SAHACHARI OFFERS
Sahachari offers two main categories of service to customers:

1. Grocery Delivery - Fresh vegetables, fruits, dairy products, spices, beverages, and packaged foods delivered to your home within 30 to 45 minutes.

2. Home Services - Bookable household services such as dishwashing and other home assistance. These can be scheduled through the app.

HOW SAHACHARI IS MANAGED
Sahachari is managed by administrators who oversee stores and delivery partners in your area. Each area has a local administrator who ensures that stores are properly stocked and that delivery partners are available. This means your orders are handled by people familiar with your local community.

STORES ON SAHACHARI
Sahachari works with verified local stores in your area. These stores are approved and managed by Sahachari administrators to ensure quality and reliability. When you shop on Sahachari, you are supporting local businesses in your community.

DELIVERY PARTNERS
Sahachari's delivery partners are registered and verified individuals who deliver orders from stores to your doorstep. Delivery partners are managed by local Sahachari administrators and are assigned to orders automatically once your order is ready for dispatch.

SERVICEABLE AREAS AND PINCODES
Sahachari serves specific pincodes in its active zones. During registration and checkout, you can enter your pincode to check if delivery is available in your area. Sahachari is expanding to new areas regularly.

ACCOUNT SECURITY
Your Sahachari account is protected by a secure login system. Your password must be at least 6 characters long. Never share your password or login details with anyone. If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, contact Sahachari support immediately through the Help section of the app.

SAHACHARI SUPPORT
If you need help with your account, orders, delivery, or any other issue, contact Sahachari support through the Help section of the app. Our team is available during business hours and typically responds within 24 hours on business days.

REGISTERING ON SAHACHARI
To register as a customer on Sahachari, you need your name, a valid email address, a mobile number, your home address, and your pincode. Your email must be unique and not already registered. Your password must be at least 6 characters. Once registered, you can log in and start shopping immediately.
