SAHACHARI STORE INFORMATION
============================

HOW SAHACHARI STORES WORK
Sahachari works with local stores and vendors in your area to deliver fresh groceries and home services to your doorstep. When you place an order, it goes directly to the store that carries your selected products. The store packs your order and hands it to a delivery partner who brings it to you.

PRODUCT AVAILABILITY
Each store on Sahachari carries its own range of products. Availability depends on what the store has in stock. If a product shows as unavailable, it may be out of stock at your local store. Product stock is updated regularly by store staff.

PRODUCT CATEGORIES
Sahachari stores carry products across several categories including fresh vegetables, fruits, dairy products, spices, beverages, packaged foods, and home services. You can filter products by category in the app to find what you need quickly.

PRODUCT PRICING
Each product has a listed price. Some products may have special offers or discounts applied, such as a percentage discount or a flat price reduction. The final price after any discount is shown clearly on the product page before you add it to your cart.

ORDER PROCESSING BY STORE
After you place an order, the store receives it and begins preparing your items. Once everything is packed and ready, the store marks your order as Ready so a delivery partner can collect it. This process typically takes a few minutes.

STORE OPERATING HOURS
Sahachari stores operate during normal business hours. Orders placed outside operating hours may take longer to process. The app will show estimated delivery times based on current store availability.

MULTIPLE STORES IN ONE ORDER
If your cart contains products from more than one store, Sahachari automatically splits your checkout into separate orders — one per store. Each order is delivered independently but they are all linked under the same shopping session so you can track them together.

PRODUCT OFFERS AND DISCOUNTS
Stores can run special offers on products from time to time. Offers may be a percentage discount (for example 10 percent off) or a flat reduction in price (for example 50 rupees off). Active offers are shown on the product page and the discounted price is applied automatically at checkout.

STOCK AVAILABILITY
Product quantities are managed by the store. If you try to order more than what is in stock, you may see a stock availability error. In that case, reduce the quantity or choose a similar product.

CONTACTING SAHACHARI ABOUT STORE ISSUES
If you received wrong items, damaged goods, or have a complaint about a store, please contact Sahachari support through the Help section of the app. We will coordinate with the store to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
